The Lantern Company

Illuminated puppet design, build and puppeteering

Working for The Lantern Company since 2011 has meant building giant illuminated puppets for a range of events such as their three day Luminous Landscapes Festival of Light, the annual Halloween Lantern Carnival, Chester Zoo Lantern Magic in the UK to name a few - and also commisioned work and performance for Aarhus Capital of Culture 2017, Standard Chartered Arts in the Park Festival in Hong Kong (2013-2018) or SOMETHING Festival in Bucharest.
